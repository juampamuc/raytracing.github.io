**Chapter 9: A Scene Testing All New Features** Let’s put it all together, with a big thin mist covering everything, and a blue subsurface reflection sphere (we didn’t implement that explicitly, but a volume inside a dielectric is what a subsurface material is). The biggest limitation left in the renderer is no shadow rays, but that is why we get caustics and subsurface for free. It’s a double-edged design decision. ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ C++ hitable *final() { int nb = 20; hitable **list = new hitable*[30]; hitable **boxlist = new hitable*[10000];
